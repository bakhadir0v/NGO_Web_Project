Our Mission

At the Canadian Wildlife Fund, our mission is to safeguard the diverse ecosystems and magnificent wildlife that call Canada home. We are dedicated to protecting endangered species, preserving natural habitats, and promoting sustainable conservation practices. Through research, education, advocacy, and on-the-ground conservation efforts, we strive to ensure the long-term survival of Canada's iconic wildlife and their habitats.
