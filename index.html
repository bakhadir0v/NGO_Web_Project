Welcome to the Canadian Wildlife Fund, where our mission is to safeguard the diverse ecosystems and magnificent wildlife that we call Canada. With your support, we work tirelessly to protect endangered species, preserve natural habitats, and promote sustainable conservation practices. Join us in our commitment to preserving Canada's natural heritage for future generations. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of our wildlife and the health of our planet. Learn more

Canadian wildlife faces numerous threats in their natural habitats. Deforestation, habitat destruction, and urbanization encroach upon their territories. Pollution, including water contamination and air pollution, poses health risks to wildlife populations and their ecosystems. Climate change exacerbates these challenges, leading to changes in migration patterns and food availability. It's imperative that we address these dangers through conservation efforts, habitat restoration, and sustainable practices to protect Canada's rich biodiversity. Learn more
